Account Deletion Information

Effective Date: June 1, 2025

At Leafie, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your personal data. You can delete your account directly from the app.

How to Delete Your Account

To delete your account, please follow these steps within the Leafie app:

  • Open the app and go to the Privacy Settings screen.
  • Select the Delete Account option.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm your account deletion.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your personal information (email, name, profile picture)
  • Plant images and metadata associated with your account
  • Subscription and purchase records
  • Any other data linked directly to your user account

Retention Period

We permanently delete your account data immediately upon confirmation of deletion. No data is retained beyond this point.

Partial Data Deletion

If you want to delete some of your data without deleting your entire account, please contact us at Leafie.helpdesk@outlook.com for assistance.
