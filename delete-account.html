Delete Your Account in Leafie

Leafie is committed to giving you full control over your data.

How to delete your account

To delete your account and all associated data, follow these steps inside the app:

  1. Open the Leafie app on your device.
  2. Go to Profile → Privacy Settings.
  3. Select “Delete Account” and confirm.

What happens when you delete your account?

  • All your personal information (email, name, etc.) will be permanently deleted.
  • Your saved plants and identification history will be removed.
  • Your data will no longer be accessible and cannot be recovered.

What we retain

  • Non-personal, anonymized analytics for performance monitoring.
  • Basic server logs retained for up to 30 days for security/fraud prevention.
