Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, name, and optionally, your profile picture.

b) Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the app, including features used and time spent on different screens.

c) Plant Images: When you upload plant photos for identification, we store these images to provide our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

a) To provide and improve our plant identification and care services

b) To personalize your experience with customized plant care recommendations

c) To communicate with you about account updates, new features, or support requests

d) To analyze usage patterns and improve the app's functionality

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes. In certain circumstances, we may share data with:

a) Service Providers: Third-party companies that help us provide our services, such as cloud storage providers

b) Legal Requirements: When required by law, legal process, or government requests

c) Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Delete your personal information
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your personal information
  • Data portability

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child, we will take steps to delete that information.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and, where appropriate, via email. We encourage you to periodically review this policy.
